What is Robotics?

A robot is a machine that can perform a complex set of tasks automatically, without human interaction. Robots are controlled by programmable computers and user sensors to interact with the environment. The basic components of a robot are the body/frame, control system, manipulators, and drivetrain.

Robotics is the branch of technology that deals with the design, construction, operation, and application of robotics. The field of robotics relies on mathematics, electronics, computer science, artificial intelligence, mechatronics, nanotechnology, bioengineering, material science, and many other fields of science and technology.

Electricity and Electronics Introduction

Voltage: An electromotive force or potential difference expressed in volts (V)

Current: The flow of electric charge, expressed in Amps (A)

Resistance: The measure of a conductors ability to resist or impede the flow of electric current, expressed in Ohms (Ω)
